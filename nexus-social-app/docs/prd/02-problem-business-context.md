## 2.1 Problems solved

| Problem | User pain | NEXUS response |
|---------|-----------|----------------|
| **Tool fatigue** | CMOs juggle 8–12 disconnected martech tools | Single workspace-scoped SoR with AI mesh + attribution |
| **Ungoverned AI** | LLMs hallucinate; MENA compliance risk | Policy tiers LOW→CRITICAL; HITL approvals; locale-aware output |
| **Data leakage** | Shadow AI blurs tenant boundaries | Supabase RLS on every tenant table |
| **Unprovable ROI** | Agencies report impressions; boards want pipeline | CRM mirror + `attribution_reports` + pilot case-study generator |
| **Slow onboarding** | Enterprise SaaS takes weeks | High-touch provision (Sprint 4/6 when unlocked) — 60s target |
| **Integration trap** | Native GA4/Meta connectors take months | CSV upload + webhooks → AI brief |

- **MENA B2B digital transformation** — Arabic/English compliant GTM demand

- **DeepSeek margin rule** — ≥55% gross margin: `(retainer - llm_cost) / retainer`
- **Capacity** — 2 pilot slots; founder-led delivery

## 2.3 Competitive positioning

NEXUS competes in **AI marketing operations / revenue intelligence** — not generic scheduling (Buffer/Hootsuite) nor full marketing cloud (HubSpot MC, Salesforce MC).

**Differentiation:**

1. 8-agent governed mesh vs single-chat copilot
2. Database-level RLS as product feature
3. Closed-loop attribution: content → CRM closed-won
4. Agency-operated delivery — clients see outcomes
5. Capital-efficient funnel integrations (CSV) vs native connector roadmaps
